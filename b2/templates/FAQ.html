FAQs

For a list of frequently asked questions, please see below. Try to search if your questions have been answered here before reaching out to us.

Is it free to register and create my own artwork ?
Yes. It's totally free. However, we always welcome donations. To donate to us, please contact us directly. For contact information, please go to the "Contact" menu.
How to register?
To register, follow the steps below.
  1. First navigate to Home page and click on the "Log in" menu at the top left.
  2. In the pop-up page, click "register".
  3. Follow the prompted steps.
How to log in?
To log in to your account, follow the steps below.
  1. First navigate to Home page and click on the "Log in" menu at the top left.
  2. In the pop-up page, enter your user name and password.
  3. Click "Log in" to log into your account.
What if I lost my password?
Please follow the following steps to retrieve your password.
  1. First navigate to Home page and click on the "Log in" menu at the top left.
  2. In the pop-up page, click "Forgot password".
  3. The system will send out a link to reset your password to your registration email.
Note: please allow up to 24 hours for the processing. If you haven't received the email after 24 hours, try to resend the link by following the above procedure, or contact us directly.
How to create my own artwork?
Please follow the following steps to create your own artwork.
  1. First navigate to Home page and click on the "Start Creating" menu at the top left.
  2. In the dropdown, click "Upload your photo". Follow the prompted steps to upload your own photo.
  3. Then go to "Select style" tab, where you can either choose a style from popular styles, or upload a style from your computer.
  4. After creating your own artwork, click "Save" tab to save it to your computer or email it to your email address.
How can save my own artwork to my computer or email to myself ?
Please follow the following steps to save your own artwork.
  1. First navigate to Home page and click on the "Start Creating" menu at the top left.
  2. In the dropdown, click "Save".
  3. Follow the prompted steps to save it to your computer or email it to your email address.
